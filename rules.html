PS: We are the official fantasy partners for Mzansi Super League 2018.

What is Fantasy gaming?

Fantasy gaming is a skill based online sports game which is classified as “game of skill”. Fantasy cricket is a part of the Fantasy sports model. It is an online game where you create a virtual team of real cricket players and score points depending on how your chosen players perform in real life matches. You have to select a match, create team, join any contest you like and win prizes. The fantasy player who scored maximum points on the leaderboard will be declared as Winner. Fantasy cricket is a game of strategy, proper cricketing skill and making correct choices.

Match

What is the difference between Upcoming, Live and Completed?

Upcoming Matches: You can only participate in Upcoming matches. These are usually updated atleast 2 days prior to the start of the match. You have to choose any match or matches in the Upcoming page to join the contests.

Live Matches: You can not join contests for any live match after the match has officially begun. If you have joined a contest for a live match, you can view the progress in the leaderboard.

Completed Matches: You can not join contests for the completed matches. If you have previously engaged in contests for a completed match, you will be able to view the winners list in the leaderboard.

How often are matches updated?

We usually update matches at least two days in advance.

What happens if a match is cancelled?

Every match has a specified number of overs which need to be bowled for it to be included in the records. If that number of overs cannot be bowled and the match is abandoned or cancelled due to reasons like poor weather or crowd disturbance, then the corresponding matches and contests in the Dotball Fantasy League would also stand cancelled.

Contest

I have joined contest. What next?

Please be sure you update your team after official announcement of playing XI. In Dotball, you can edit your team until the first ball of the match is bowled. After that you can check your leaderboard during the match or after the end of the match.

I am unable to join contests for live match. Why?

Once the first bowl is bowled, the match will be declared as live and all contests become closed for entry. Hence, you can’t join in any contest for that live match.

No contests are open for this upcoming match. Why?

We are yet to update contests for this match. If the match is operational, contests will be added shortly.

I have won a contest. When will I receive the prize?

The prizes will be disbursed to the winners within one month from the conclusion of the concerned match.

Teams

How should I create my team?

After registering your Dotball account, click on the ‘Create Team’ option for the match you’d like to join. Create Your team of 11 players including Captain and Vice-Captain for the match you want to join by using an allocated budget of 100 credits. You can select upto 1 wicketkeeper, 3 to 5 batsmen, 3 to 5 bowlers and upto 4 all rounders. Also, you have to select atleast one rookie and between 1 and 4 overseas players.

How many teams can I create?

You can create upto one team for a particular match.

How often are points updated?

The scores (or points) are updated by us every few minutes in the backend! However, the final points and rank of your team are updated atleast four hours after the match is over. After the match, the winners are announced.

Points are not getting updated. What should I do?

This could be an issue with the third-party scoring feed. You can be rest assured this issue will be resolved before running settlements. You can still reach out to us using the ‘Contact Us’ tab in your profile.

What if there is a tie for the same position in leaderboard?

If two or more teams have the same points after a match, then the prize for the position at which they are tied would be given to all such teams.

Email ID not verified. Did not receive OTP. Why?

This could be due to two reasons.

1. First, check your spam folder to see if mails from Dotball are being redirected there. If so, you need to click on the ‘This is not spam’ button of your email page.

2. The second reason could be that you used a different email id for Dotball registration than the one you are checking now. If you are unable to login despite entering the correct email address for verification, please reach out to us at ‘Contact Us’ tab in your profile.

Why is Smart ID card required for verification?

Smart ID card serves as necessary identity proof for user and address verification. If you do not have your Smart ID Card, the verification process will be left incomplete and you wouldn’t be able to receive prizes.

How long does it take for Smart ID card to get verified?

Your Smart ID card gets verified within 4 working days.

Why did my Smart ID card get rejected?

This can happen due to some reasons such as,

  • 1) Smart ID card number entered by you is not matching the uploaded Smart ID card.
  • 2) Name entered by you is not matching the uploaded Smart ID card.
  • 3) DOB entered by you is not matching the uploaded Smart ID card.
  • 4) Uploaded image is not a valid Smart ID card.
  • 5) Uploaded image of Smart ID card is not clear.
  • 6) Smart ID card already registered in system.

When are teams locked in for every match on Dotball?

We understand that fantasy players want to be on the same page when the playing XI is announced. Hence, on Dotball, they can pick players or edit their teams as per real playing XI. The teams are locked for most matches before the first ball has been bowled. We do not wish to place constraints on the user and increase the element of chance. Fantasy cricket is data driven and we want to increase that percentage.
