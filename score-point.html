General Points:

(i) Captain: All attributed points will be multiplied by X2 times for the assigned virtual captain.

(ii) Vice-Captain: All attributed points will be multiplied by X1.5 times for the assigned virtual Vice-captain.

(iii) Substitute: 0 Points
*However, Concussion Substitutes (as defined in the Cricket Australia Playing Conditions for Domestic Matches) will be granted points for their contribution, governed by the Cricket Australia Playing Conditions for Domestic Matches.

Transferred player: 0 Points
*If a real-life player is transferred in the period between scheduled updates, such a transfer will not be reflected in the list of players until the next update. It’s to be noted that while the transferred player will be available for selection in the transferred squad’s roster of players in a contest during the intervening period, no points will be assigned to that player during that contest.
